Status:

Comments:
hackmastera at 2020-05-12 15:33:37 :

Some fields, like metal are pure description. Some, like obverse_legend, are clearly transcribed and seem like they shouldn't be transformed in this way.

edge has a little of both; values in production are: "serrated", "plain", "FIFTY CENTS OR HALF A DOLLAR", "PAX UNA TRIUMPHIS INNUMERIS POTIOR", "reX regVM consVLta DeVs fortVnet VbIqVe (=1691)", "milled", "GOTT MIT UNS", "smooth with arabesques", "beaded border", "**ONE**DOLLAR**OR**UNIT*** UNDRED CENTS**", "**ONE**DOLLAR**OR**UNIT***HUNDRED CENTS**", "**ONE**DOLLAR**OR**UNIT***HUNDR*D CENTS**", "inscription: AP", "inscribed (see description)", "reeded"

Many of thse, like "serrated" and "plain", which are common values, seem like they should be capitalized. But "reX regVM consVLta DeVs fortVnet VbIqVe (=1691)" seems like it shouldn't be. For now I lean towards not including this field.

obverse_figure has a mix of capitalized proper nouns like "Pan" and uncapitalized nouns like "arms". Leaning towards including this field.
