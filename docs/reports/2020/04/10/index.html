#173 Homepage Redesign

Creator:
Amanda Ferrara
Created:
2020-03-18 19:13:14
Status:
Open
Comments:
Shaun Ellis at 2020-04-10 17:14:55 :

Notes from our 4/10 discussion:

Feature Digital Content should be prioritized over Featured Blog Posts (should be changed from Recent Updates).

Locations and Hours should be limited to just Mudd and Firestone reading rooms.

"Search the Finding Aids" rather than just "Finding Aids"

Digitized Content could be curated or automated. Automated would be easiest, could be most recent, or randomly generated from what we have. How would thumbnails be automated? Curation would require more work on the part of SC staff, but could result in more compelling content that the dept wants to showcase. Need to have this conversation with AnnaLee, Squirrel, and others who may be responsible for this work.

Advanced Search feature? Will basic search support boolean searching of multiple topics and phrases? Does the current approach of facet refinement support these boolean style features? This is a good topic for User Research. We should come up with "advanced search tasks" and see if facet refinement is enough for people or if they struggle.

No one uses Locations. We want to retain Topics and Names as a feature, although they are outdated and ADAPT needs to revisit and revise. Are people actually using these features or not? Action item: Shaun will get some numbers on these. Need a way to "Browse Collections" that does not involve a blank search.

Happened upon potential blogs to pull content from and these are the top contenders: PUL Manuscripts News, Mudd Manuscript Library Blog, Notabilia (though related to rare books, so perhaps not), RBSC News & Events (though hasn't been updated in quite a while and is a little boring, so maybe not).
