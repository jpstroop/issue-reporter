#14 Geographic headings that appear in other headings need to be updated

Creator:
mzelesky
Created:
2020-04-08 19:32:33
Status:
Open
Comments:
M Chen at 2020-04-17 13:27:44 :

If I understand the issue correctly, there is a way to take care of at least some of the cases. In the MARC record of "Kyïv (Ukraine)" we have these fields: 151##$a Kyïv (Ukraine) 451##$w nne $a Kiev (Ukraine) 781#0 $z Ukraine $z Kyïv

Note that 781 is the correct form to be used in 650 for geographical subdivision. The pathway to convert $z Kiev into $z Kyïv looks like below--

For "650#0…$z Ukraine $z Kiev…" i.e., ["650#0…$z A $z B…"] Convert to: Kiev (Ukraine) i.e., [B (A)] Compare with 451, if there is a match Convert to 781: "650#0…$z Ukraine $z Kyïv…"

M Chen at 2020-04-17 14:09:38 :

This solution/pathway is limited to "some of the cases" because if it is a US geographical name, the AB -->B(A) conversion would not be as straightforward. For example, 151##$a Princeton (N.J.) 781#0$z New Jersey $z Princeton

See https://www.loc.gov/marc/authority/ad781.html as well as SHM: H 810 Qualification of Geographic Headings http://desktop.loc.gov/search?view=document&id=Infobasescmshm0Dash0Dash0Dash294&fq=coreresources|true#

Creator:

Created:

Status:

Comments:
Esmé Cowles at 2020-04-17 18:43:41 :

It looks like boxless ephemera do not require measurements — I think the plan was to have born digital items not be in boxes, and we changed the required fields based on that.

Kim Leaman at 2020-04-17 18:56:52 :

@escowles . Thanks for the information! Ellen (SAE subject Selector) and I talked a lot about boxes versus collections and it was decided the virtual boxes with dummy barcodes would be the best way forward. I share our rationale here, as well as the proposed workflow. If there is any downside and it would, indeed, be preferable to not use boxes, please let me know! :)

  • Decision: create dummy boxes (use "00000000000000" for the barcodes, since they will be required) — those boxes won't show up in dpul, but can be used in figgy to segment the items for management

  • Rationale: Creation of dummy boxes versus adding collections would simplify ingest, and diminish potential for human error when adding to/deciding on collections in the future.

  • Workflow: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-k8jQuP8DbUVFjQZ0srz2PSA2EJ7rI4sqwn52IwpNYs/edit

Esmé Cowles at 2020-04-17 19:26:19 :

It seems like there are a couple of different options here to resolve these two competing needs: 1. Have collections that don't show up in DPUL (maybe draft vs. complete?) 2. Have boxes that are "virtual" boxes whose members don't require dimensions 3. Have some new kind of grouping in ephemera projects for born-digital collections
