#642 Determine how holdings with no items behave in Alma

Creator:
Kate Lynch
Created:
2020-04-23 14:24:25
Status:
Open
Comments:
hackartisan at 2020-12-01 19:16:07 :

In voyager, if there's a holding with no item we retrieve the order status. In Alma there will be an item on the holding. If there is a real barcode it's an item. If there's a "temporary" barcode it's provisional (e.g. on order).

Real barcodes all begin with 32101 and are codabar formatted. The others will have a prefix (the barcode will start with text).
