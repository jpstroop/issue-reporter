#2151 Update list of indigenous languages

Creator:
Jane Sandberg
Created:
2023-05-05 18:59:54
Status:
Open
Comments:
M Chen at 2023-05-12 15:02:00 :

I did find some odd cases where bibs should be tagged as "Indigenous Languages (Western Hemisphere)" but are not.

First, it looks as though $v Texts is not working properly.

Among the results of Algonquian languages—Texts (19) 6 records are not tagged

None of Apache languages—Texts (3) is tagged.

Among the results of Zapotec language—Texts (42) 7 records are not tagged

Now I know Blacklight does not distinguish between $v Texts and $x Texts, and for our tagging interest we are only interested in the former, but spot-checking found plenty of $v Texts in there.

Or take a look at the title Bitter water: Diné oral histories of the Navajo-Hopi land dispute (4 records) All four have the identical string 650 #0 a| Navajo language v| Texts. But only the three with 041 a| nav are tagged. (One of them is a Harvard copy. I visited hollis.harvard.edu to find its source marc record) MMSID 99125348431306421 has 041 a| eng only, and is not tagged as Indigenous Languages.

Second, my analysis also exposes some encoding errors in the 041 fields of bib records. Lumped constructions like

  • 041 $a algeng
  • 041 $a engspanah

may be found in older records (and I suspect a higher presence in SCSB records), and would be skipped by the tagging project. Would it be possible to loosen up the matching criteria a tiny bit to scoop up such records as well?
