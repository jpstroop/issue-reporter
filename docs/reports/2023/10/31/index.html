James R. Griffin III at 2023-10-31 17:40:21 :

It appears that the apache-tomcat service, which was set to the runlevels required to automatically start during the booting of the server, was in conflict with a similar service dspace. The former has been disabled, while the latter has been explicitly enabled:

$ sudo systemctl disable apache-tomcat.service
$ sudo systemctl enable dspace.service

I am applying these changes to the staging and dev environments.
