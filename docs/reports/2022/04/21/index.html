mzelesky at 2022-04-21 19:05:49 :

Draft procedure for calculating a due date:

First, no due date can be after the patron's expiration date.

For UGRD Undergraduate and REG Regular Staff: The new due date is 56 days from the day the job runs, or the date of the patron's expiration.

For SENR Senior Undergraduate: The new due date is May 10th, or the date of the patron's expiration. If today's date is after May, it is May 10th of the next year. If before May 10th, it is May 10th of the current year.

For GRAD Graduate Student and P Faculty & Professional: The new due date is June 15th, or the date of the patron's expiration. If today's date is after April 30th, it is June 15th of the next year. If before April 30th, it is June 15th of the current year.

I believe those are the only patron groups eligible for SCSB partner materials for now. I will get confirmation from Joan.

We can also run all those dates by Joan to make sure they're correct.
