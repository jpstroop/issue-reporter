  • ruby (which depends on common)
  • deploy_user (which also depends on common)
  • mariadb (which depends on deploy_user ((which in turn depends on common)) )
  • redis (no dependencies!!!)
  • sidekiqworker (which depends on railsapp ((which in turn depends on common, bind9, deploy_user, passenger, postgresql, nodejs. . . most of which have dependencies of their own)) )
