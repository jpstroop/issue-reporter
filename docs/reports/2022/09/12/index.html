#3 Describe: Minimal Metadata List

Creator:
Hannah H
Created:
2022-08-30 21:59:33
Status:
Open
Comments:
Hannah H at 2022-09-12 19:31:18 :

Creating a checklist of what we have applied currently. (Note: What Datacite has made recommended or optional can be mandatory for us locally. I will bold the properties that are mandatory for us.)

Datacite Mandatory

  • [x] 1 Identifier (This is the ORCID ID and will be visible to users)
  • [x] 1.a identifierType (This is ORCID and will not be visible to users)
  • [x] 2 Creator (The depositor can make additional creator fields to enter multiple creators)
  • [x] 2.1 creatorName (This information is what becomes visible in the "Creator(s)" field)
  • [x] 3 Title (This is visible to users)
  • [x] 4 Publisher (This is visible to users and defaults to Princeton University. It may be manually changed)
  • [x] 5 PublicationYear
  • [ ] 10 ResourceType
  • [ ] 10.a resourceTypeGeneral

Datacite Recommended or Optional

  • [ ] 8 Date
  • [ ] 8.a dateType
  • [ ] 11 AlternateIdentifier
  • [ ] 11.a alternateIdentifierType
  • [ ] 12 RelatedIdentifier
  • [ ] 12.a relatedIdentifierType
  • [ ] 12.b relationType
  • [ ] 15 Version
  • [x] 16 Rights
  • [x] 17 Description
  • [x] 17.a descriptionType
  • [ ] 19 FundingReference
  • [ ] 19.1 funderName
  • [ ] 20 RelatedItem
  • [ ] 20.a relatedItemType
  • [ ] 20.b relationType
