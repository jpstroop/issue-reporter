Creator:

Bess Sadler at 2022-07-08 15:28:53 :

The problem is because the titles in the embargo spreadsheet do not match the titles from ThesisCentral.

For example: title1 = "Breaking the Species Barrier_ Investigating the Host Proteins Involved in Human and Murine Hepatitis - Mansi Totwani.xml" title2 = "Breaking the Species Barrier: Investigating the Host Proteins Involved in Human and Murine Hepatitis B Infection"

We have to compare titles because a given netid might have more than one thesis, so when applying an embargo we have to match on both netid and thesis title. We are already normalizing the titles for punctuation and capitalization and comparing the Levenshtein distance. I'm now also going to truncate the title at 50 characters. Hopefully that should be enough to solve the problem while still detecting places where it should NOT apply an embargo.
