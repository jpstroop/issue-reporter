Kate Lynch at 2022-05-12 19:06:17 :

This is due to the fact that there are terms missing from the Japanese language translation files associated with OJS.

According to documentation, OJS language translations are entirely community-created and many of them are partially incomplete. A list of languages supported in the program and their completeness of translation can be seen here -

https://translate.pkp.sfu.ca/projects/ojs/#languages

PKP is very grateful for help making translations more complete; here is the page on their website detailing where and how translations can be contributed to the project - https://pkp.sfu.ca/contributors/translation/

If there is bandwidth for this, I would suggest that the Japanese language translation for OJS be expanded and contributed back to the project. In the short term to remove the visible error, the menu itself could be updated to remove the "Announcements" page in favor of a different link if desired.

I'm happy to discuss the path forward further and/or demo what I saw investigating this!
