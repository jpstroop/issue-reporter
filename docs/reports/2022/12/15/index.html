#708 Generate the OAR Usage Metrics Report for 2022

Creator:
James R. Griffin III
Created:
2022-12-13 21:28:47
Status:
Open
Comments:
James R. Griffin III at 2022-12-15 14:46:10 :

I have identified the correct series of Solr Queries for generating this report:

## Items (Views)

curl "http://localhost:8080/solr/statistics/select?indent=on&version=2.2&start=0&rows=1&fl=*&wt=json&explainOther=&hl.fl=&facet=true&start=0&q=owningComm%3A$COMM_ID%20AND%20owningColl%3A$COLL_ID%20AND%20isBot%3Afalse%20AND%20time%3A$QUERY_TIME%20AND%20type%3A2"

## Bitstreams (Downloads)

curl "http://localhost:8080/solr/statistics/select?indent=on&version=2.2&start=0&rows=1&fl=*&wt=json&explainOther=&hl.fl=&facet=true&start=0&q=owningComm%3A$COMM_ID%20AND%20owningColl%3A$COLL_ID%20AND%20isBot%3Afalse%20AND%20time%3A$QUERY_TIME%20AND%20type%3A0"

...where $COMM_ID is the Community ID, $COLL_ID is the Collection ID, and $QUERY_TIME is the Solr datestamp for the time range. The type parameter may either be 0 for Bitstreams (downloads), or 2 for Items (views).
