Here's the story from more logs examination:

  • the load balancer sent the request to dpul, waited around for a response
  • nginx on the dpul machine received the request, routed, it, and waited for rails to respond
  • the app started fetching the manifest from figgy
  • nginx on the dpul machine didn't get a response quickly enough, and returned a 499
  • the load balancer served a 405 error (still not sure why a 405)
  • the app got the manifest and enqueued the job, returning a 302 redirect to... nobody because the nginx service wasn't really listening anymore

The job was enqueued at 9:15, 10:15, and 12:02. 2 of them are still on the queue but one was performed between 11:07 and 11:45 (ironically right before the last one was enqueued).
